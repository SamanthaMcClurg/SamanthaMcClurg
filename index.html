About

Samantha McClurg is a multi-award winning screen and stage actor based in Queensland, Australia. Sam has been studying and working professionally as an actor since 2014. Some of her recent film credits include the leading role of Mary O'Shea in A Godless Country, for which she has currently won 6 Best Actress Awards, as well as the supporting roles of Kellie in Frank's and Arabelle in Take It Back Now. She has also had theatrical roles in Cosi, The Butler Did It, Pack of Lies, and The Naked Truth. Earlier this year Samantha played Jen in Gold Coast Little Theatre's production of The Cake, which was the Australian Premiere of the play, and in May 2022 you can catch her in the role of Meg McGrath in Tugun Theatre's production of Crimes of the Heart.

Directed by Jesse Richardson. 2020.
